3.  Customer service and insurance

All leading sales producers managed to reach the top and stay there because of the patronage and support of their existing clients that helped them build their business. A large part of their income comes from the commissions for renewal of the contracts. Their clients are also the source for acquiring new customers.

Secret of their success is commitment to serving their customers.

Keeping a customer happy benefit the agent and the company due to customer’s lifetime value.

 

Customer lifetime value may be defined as the sum of economic benefits that can be derived from building a sound relationship with a customer over a long period of time.
