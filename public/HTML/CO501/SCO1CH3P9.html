Chapter 3                                Grievance Redressal Mechanism

 

 

 

‘Consumer Forum’

Under the Act, 3 levels of ‘Consumer Fora’ (‘fora’ is the plural of ‘forum’) were established –

Level

Forum

Remarks

1.

District Forum

a) can entertain disputes which fall under its territory

     (geographical limit)

b) can entertain disputes up to an amount of Rs. 20

     lacks (financial limit)

c) an appeal against its award can be made within        30 days from the date of award

2.

State Commission

a) any claim above Rs. 20 lacks & up to Rs. 1 Crore

b) entertains appeals against the order of District          Forum

c) can call for papers for any consumer dispute     pending with District Forum

d) an appeal against its award may be preferred to the National Commission within 30 days of the date of award.

3.

National Commission

a) Final & the highest authority established under the   Act

b) compensation exceeds Rs. 1 Crore

c) deals with appeals made against the order of any State Commission

d) an appeal can be made in Supreme Court against the award within 30 days.
  • All the 3 fora are quasi-judicial authorities and have powers of a Civil Court.

 

The procedure for filing a complaint is very simple in all the above three Redressal agencies. There is no fee for filing a complainant or filing an appeal either before the state commission or national commission. The complaint can be filed by the complainant himself or by his authorized agent.
