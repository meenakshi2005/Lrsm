Chapter 1                                                 Introduction to Insurance

 

Hazard

 

The condition or conditions which increase the probability of a loss or its severity, and thus impact(s) the risk is / are known as hazard. When insurers make an assessment of the risk, it is generally with reference to the hazards to which the asset is subject to.
