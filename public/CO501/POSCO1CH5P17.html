Chapter 5                                                                  Miscellaneous

 

Procedure:

 

From the side of a complainant:

·         A complaint should be filed in writing in the appropriate Forum within the time limit

·         The appropriate Forum means, the complaint should be filed in District, State or National Commission depending upon the territorial limits & financial limits (as specified in the table above) of the case.

·         The maximum time limit for filing is 2 years from the date on which the cause of action has arisen.  (However, the Forum has the authority to accept complaints beyond the stipulated period, if it is convinced that there is sufficient reason on the part of the complainant for not filing a complaint within the time limit)

·         A complaint can be filed by the complainant himself or by his authorized agents

·         NO advocate is required for filing a complaint

·         There is NO fee for filing a complaint

 

From the side of the Forum:

·         On receipt of a complaint the District Forum, the State Commission or the National Commission, as the case may be, shall send a copy of the complaint to the opposite party directing him to give his version within a period of 30 days (or such extended period as may be granted by the Forum)

·          On receipt of the reply or if the opposite party remains absent, the Forum on the basis of evidence may decide the matter. 

·         The Forum can summon witnesses, production of documents, receiving of evidence on affidavit etc as it has the powers of a Civil Court

·         The Forum can also follow principles of natural justice

·         Every order passed by the Forum shall be final (if no appeal has been preferred against the said order).
