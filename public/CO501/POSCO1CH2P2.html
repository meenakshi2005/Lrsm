Chapter 2                                                 Indian Insurance Market

 

2 – Agents:

Individual Insurance Agents (Individual Financial Planner/ Independent Advisor / Financial Consultants) are the most important marketing channels for insurance business.

 

They are appointed by the insurers as per the IRDAI. (Appointment of Agents) Guidelines, 2015 as per the Board Approved Policy laying down the procedure for appointment of agents, their qualifications and disqualifications, suspension. Enquiry, cancellation of appointment, inspection by IRDAI, code of conducts etc.

 

Separate appointments for Life, Non-life, Health Insurance and mono line insurers are allowed but can work with only one insurer in each class.

 

The appointment is valid for 3 years, unless terminated or surrendered earlier.

A Composite Agent works for both Life Insurer and Non-life (General) Insurer or Health Insurer.

 

 

3- Intermediaries:

a) CORPORATE  AGENTS:

Insurers can appoint corporate bodies, like banks, companies, firms, cooperative societies as  Corporate Agents. Corporate Agents are also governed by the IRDAI.  Banc assurance (banks functioning as Corporate Agents) will emerge as a very important channel for distribution of insurance products.

Corporate Agents can solicit and procure life, general and health insurance business of maximum of three insurers at a time. CAs have to appoint Principal Officer and Specified Persons, who have to undergo 75/50 hours of  Online Ttraining through Insurance Institute of India and pass the test to solicit an procure insurance business.

 

CAs are governed by IRDAI (Registration of Corporate Agents) Regulations 2015 effective from 1st April 2016. Registration is valid for 3 years. Application for renewal can be made 90 days in advance. Renewal is allowed after 25 hours of renewal training but no examination is reqired to be passed.

 

b) BUSINESS CORRESPONDENTS OF BANKS:

Banks have been permitted to appoint business correspondents in rural area and they can distribute Micro Insurance Products, subject to certain conditions.
